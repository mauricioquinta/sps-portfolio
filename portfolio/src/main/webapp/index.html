About Me: a short history

My name is Mauricio. I was born in San Miguel, El Salvador 1998. I moved to the states, thanks to my dad, at age 7. I grew up in northern Virginia less than an hour from DC. I was a curious little kid, first I figured out how to speak english, then progressed with the childhood thing. In middle school the tech-ed teacher said 'hey wanna build robots after school and stuff?' and that sounded dope so I started doing that. In highschool I liked history and physics the most but math more than english. I got really into robotics and engineering in general, it seemed right up my alley. I love problem solving, puzzles, and building things. I was particularly keen on programming! So I got myself into an engineering school and decided to pursue a computer science degree, so here I am living the dream... as my family's designated tech guy! Whooo!
